## Dashboard Features

### Quick Stats

The stats panel shows at-a-glance metrics:
- **Total Customers**: All customers for the selected week
- **Active**: Customers with active engagement
- **At Risk**: Customers needing attention (Health Score 60-79)
- **Critical**: Customers requiring immediate action (Health Score < 60)

### Interactive Features

- **Search**: Type to find customers by name
- **Status Filter**: Filter by Production, Pre-Production, Planning, etc.
- **Engagement Filter**: Show only Active, At Risk, or Critical
- **Health Filter**: Filter by health score ranges
- **Sort Options**: Sort by name, health score, or engagement level

### Customer Cards

Each card displays:
- Company name and logo
- Current status badge
- Engagement level indicator
- Health score with visual bar
- MAU (Monthly Active Users) metric
- TTIV (Time to Initial Value) metric
- Engagement summary
- Blockers alert (if applicable)
- Industry and ESE Lead information
- Data-driven insights (click to expand)

---

## Data Insights

Click "View Data Insights" on any customer card to see:

- **Synopsis**: Data-driven summary of engagement patterns
- **Key Observations**: Important data points and metrics
- **Trend Indicators**: Historical patterns and momentum

## Using This Dashboard

1. **Quick Scan**: Use the stats panel to identify priorities
2. **Filter Down**: Apply filters to focus on specific customer groups
3. **Search**: Find specific customers by name
4. **Review Details**: Click cards to expand and see full insights
5. **Take Action**: Use observations to inform customer success strategies
